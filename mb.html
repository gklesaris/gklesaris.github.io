Midnight Breakfast

It takes coordination from numerous different Stevens departments for this event to be successful. Greg acts as the main liaison between the Entertainment Committee, Stevens Dining, The Office of Residence Life, and the Office of Student Life to make sure every party is staying to their scope and schedule. During the event, each department is responsible for different parts of the event, while Greg and the Entertainment Committee make sure everyone works together to make sure the students enjoy the experience. Everyone from student to Vice President helps at the event, as can be seen by Assistant Vice President for Student Affairs and General Manager of Stevens Dining prepare Hanukkah latkes for students to celebrate the first night of Hanukkah.
