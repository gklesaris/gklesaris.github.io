Yacht Sea Trial Testing

Completing a towing tank model test is one of the most important steps in Naval Architecture and Engineering. Stevens is revolutionary in the Naval Architecture field because of its 312 foot long Davidson Lab Towing Tank. Greg Klesaris is currently working on his thesis where he will be comparing different prediction methods of hull resistance, and comparing those predictions with real-world sea trial results.
