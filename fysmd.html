First Year Student Move-In Day

The Stevens Move-In Day Staff is made up of both Resident Assistants and Orientation Leaders. As a third year Resident Assistant, Greg Klesaris is in charge of the operation of the entrance and first residence hall, making sure traffic flows smoothly and students moving into the first residence hall unload efficiently. Greg and fellow RA made sure that each car was unloaded by helpers in less than 60 seconds from arrival, to make move-in day as stress-free to families as possible.
